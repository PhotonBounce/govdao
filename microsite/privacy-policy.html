Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GOVDAO ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the GOVDAO mobile application and this website (collectively, the "Service"). GOVDAO is a non-custodial Ethereum governance client. We have designed the Service to minimise the collection of personal data. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

Wallet Address. When you connect an Ethereum wallet to GOVDAO, your public wallet address is read client-side for the sole purpose of displaying your on-chain member role, voting history, and governance delegations. Your wallet address is not transmitted to or stored on any GOVDAO server.

On-Chain Transaction Data. All votes, proposals, and governance actions you perform through GOVDAO are recorded directly on the Ethereum blockchain. This data is public by nature and exists independently of GOVDAO. We do not create, modify, or control blockchain records.

Usage Analytics (Optional). We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed, feature usage frequency) using privacy-preserving methods that do not identify individual users. No wallet address or IP address is linked to these statistics.

Device Information. Standard device metadata (operating system version, app version) may be collected automatically for crash reporting purposes only. This data is not linked to your wallet address or any personal identifier.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display your on-chain governance status, role, and voting record within the app.
  • To render real-time proposal data, quorum calculations, and delegate analytics sourced from the Ethereum network.
  • To diagnose technical errors and improve app stability.
  • We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

GOVDAO does not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or government-issued ID.
  • Your private key, seed phrase, or any wallet credentials.
  • Your geographic location.
  • Any financial account information beyond the public on-chain record.

4. Third-Party Services

Ethereum RPC Providers. GOVDAO queries the Ethereum blockchain through RPC providers such as Infura and Alchemy. When the app makes a blockchain query, your network request is routed through these providers. The data transmitted is limited to the blockchain query itself (e.g., reading a smart contract state or fetching transaction history for a given address). Please review the privacy policies of Infura and Alchemy for information about how they handle RPC request metadata.

No Other Third-Party Data Sharing. We do not share information with analytics platforms, advertising networks, social media companies, or any other third parties beyond the RPC providers described above.

5. Data Storage and Retention

GOVDAO operates as a non-custodial application. We do not maintain user accounts and store no personal data off-chain. App preferences (such as display settings) are stored locally on your device using standard platform storage (AsyncStorage / device keychain) and never transmitted to our servers.

Because we store no personal data, there is no retention period to define and no centralised database to purge.

6. Blockchain Disclaimer

All votes, delegation actions, and on-chain governance transactions you submit through GOVDAO are permanently and publicly recorded on the Ethereum blockchain. This is an inherent property of blockchain technology and not specific to GOVDAO. These records are immutable and cannot be deleted, altered, or concealed by GOVDAO or any other party.

By using GOVDAO to interact with governance contracts, you acknowledge that your wallet address and all associated on-chain actions are permanently public.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR and CCPA

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or California, you have certain data rights. Because GOVDAO stores no personal data on our systems, many of these rights are satisfied by the architecture of the Service itself:

  • Right to Access: We hold no personal data about you. Your on-chain record is publicly readable at any Ethereum block explorer.
  • Right to Rectification: We hold no personal data to correct.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Not applicable with respect to blockchain data. The Ethereum blockchain is a public, immutable ledger. On-chain records cannot be deleted by GOVDAO or any third party. We store no off-chain personal data that could be erased.
  • Right to Data Portability: Your on-chain data is already publicly accessible and portable via any Ethereum node or block explorer.
  • Right to Object / Opt Out of Sale: We do not sell personal data. California residents: we do not sell or share your personal information as defined under the CCPA/CPRA.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any privacy rights.

8. Children's Privacy

GOVDAO is not directed at children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from persons under 18. If you believe a minor has accessed the Service and provided personal data, please contact us so we can investigate.

9. Security

We employ industry-standard security practices for the infrastructure that supports this website and the app distribution pipeline. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. Because we do not store personal data on our servers, the primary security considerations rest with your own wallet security practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notification or a prominent notice on this website. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision. Continued use of the Service after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
