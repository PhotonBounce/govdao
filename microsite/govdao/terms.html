2. Description of Service

GOVDAO provides a mobile and web interface for interacting with governance smart contracts deployed on the Polygon blockchain. Features include:

  • Viewing and creating governance proposals
  • Casting on-chain and off-chain votes
  • Monitoring treasury balances and movements
  • Managing organization members and delegates
  • Scheduling and executing guardian drills (Premium)
  • Accessing governance analytics and reporting (Premium)

The App is a read/write interface. It does not custody funds or hold governance power on your behalf.
