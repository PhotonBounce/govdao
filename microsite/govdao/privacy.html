Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 21, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 21, 2026

Short version: GOVDAO does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. The app reads governance state directly from the Polygon blockchain through your own RPC connection. No backend account is required. No email, name, or personal information is ever requested.

1. Who We Are

GOVDAO is a mobile governance application developed by PhotonBounce. The app enables member-led organizations to propose, vote on, queue, and execute governance decisions using five smart contracts deployed on the Polygon mainnet blockchain.

Questions about this policy may be sent to: lindapots2000@gmail.com

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Your wallet seed phrase, private keys, or credentials
  • Your IP address or device identifiers (beyond what Expo/React Native platform SDKs may log locally)
  • Your location data
  • Any biometric data — biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave via the OS; we receive only a boolean success/failure

3. Blockchain Data

All on-chain reads (proposals, votes, treasury balances, member registry entries) are fetched directly from the Polygon network via an RPC endpoint configured in the app. This data is public by nature — it exists on a permissionless blockchain and is readable by anyone. We do not proxy or log these requests on our servers.

4. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed by Google Play Billing and optionally managed through RevenueCat. We receive only an entitlement status (active/expired) — no payment card details or financial information reaches our application code. Purchase history is governed by Google's and RevenueCat's own privacy policies.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable governance reminders, a push token is registered with Expo's notification service. This token is stored on your device and used solely to deliver governance-event notifications you have subscribed to. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

6. Advertising

The free tier may display banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Upgrading to Premium removes all advertising.

7. Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (no Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.). The governance health-score and analytics features shown in the app are computed locally from on-chain data fetched at runtime.

8. Deep Links

The app registers the govdao:// URI scheme for deep links (e.g., direct navigation to a proposal). These links are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

9. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • WalletConnect / Coinbase Wallet — wallet connection protocols. See their respective privacy policies.
  • Google AdMob — advertising on free tier. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Expo / Alchemy — app runtime and RPC access. See their respective policies.

10. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. On-chain data is immutable by design — that's the blockchain guarantee that makes governance transparent.

11. Children's Privacy

GOVDAO is not directed at children under 13. The app is a governance tool for member-led organizations and requires wallet connectivity inappropriate for minors. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above and, if material, noted in the app's release notes.

13. Contact

For any privacy questions or data requests: lindapots2000@gmail.com
