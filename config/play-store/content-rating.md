## Notes for Reviewer

GOVDAO is a governance tool for member-led decentralized organizations. Users interact with Ethereum smart contracts to vote on proposals, manage treasury spending, and maintain membership registries. There is no gambling, no real-money trading within the app, and no user-generated content beyond on-chain governance text submitted to public blockchains.

The app does reference "smart contract risk" in its terms of service as a required disclosure for any blockchain application, but this is a legal disclaimer — not a depiction of financial risk activities within the app UI.
