## Full Description (4000 chars max)

GOVDAO puts on-chain governance in your pocket. Built for member-led organizations that need transparent, auditable decision-making without the complexity.

**What GOVDAO Does**

GOVDAO connects directly to your organization's deployed governance smart contracts — Governor, Treasury, MemberRegistry, Timelock, and EmergencyGuardian — and lets every member participate from their phone.

**Vote On-Chain**
Cast binding votes on governance proposals from your wallet. Every vote is recorded on-chain with a cryptographic receipt you can verify any time. Supports WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet, and injected wallets.

**Create & Run Proposals**
Draft and submit proposals, queue succeeded ones into the timelock, and execute them — the full governance lifecycle from your phone, confirmed with Face ID.

**Proposal Risk Analyzer**
Before you vote, see a 0–100 risk score across five factors — treasury impact, deadline urgency, quorum shortfall, proposer trust, and upgrade risk — with a plain-English recommendation.

**Delegate Power Map**
Visualize how voting power flows through your DAO. See whales, active delegates, and dormant holders at a glance in an interactive delegation map.

**Sentiment Pulse**
Read the room with live community reactions on every proposal, aggregated into a clear bullish-to-bearish sentiment score.

**Treasury Oversight**
View your DAO's treasury balance, spending caps, and pending timelocked requests in real time. Submit spend requests that respect the required delay before execution.

**Emergency Guardian**
Monitor the EmergencyGuardian contract and rehearse guardian drills so your safety mechanisms are always ready. (Premium)

**Analytics Dashboard**
Track participation rates, pass/fail ratios, quorum distance, and top delegate performance across your governance history. (Premium)

**Deploy Wizard**
A step-by-step wizard for bootstrapping a new governance instance — MemberRegistry, Timelock, Governor, Treasury, and EmergencyGuardian — from one guided flow. (Premium)

**Activity Feed**
A chronological feed of every governance event: votes, proposals, treasury movements, guardian drills, and member changes. Filter by type or date, export to CSV or JSON. (Premium export)

**Governance Health & Reputation**
A single 0–100 health score grades your DAO's vitals, and you earn achievement badges and reputation tiers as you participate. A 12-week activity heatmap shows your contribution streak.

**Insights**
A delegate power map of voting influence, a proposal risk analyzer, and a sentiment pulse on the membership's mood.

**Calendar, Reminders & Templates**
A governance calendar with chain-anchored countdowns, optional local reminders before deadlines, and ready-made proposal templates that pre-fill the form so you can move fast.

**Treasury Allocation**
See exactly how the treasury is allocated across ETH, stablecoins, and tokens, with percentage shares and total value.

**Secure & Personal**
Confirm on-chain actions with Face ID / fingerprint, deep-link straight to a proposal, and tune sound, motion, and accessibility to your taste.

**Free & Premium**
GOVDAO is free, supported by ads. Upgrade to Premium to remove ads and unlock the analytics dashboard, deploy wizard, guardian drills, and activity export — billed through Google Play.

**Demo Mode**
Not connected to a live chain yet? GOVDAO runs in demo mode with realistic sample data so you can explore every feature before deploying contracts.

**Privacy First**
- The app collects no name, email, or personal data
- Wallet addresses are used on-chain only, through your own contract
- No backend servers — reads state directly from your RPC endpoint
- All transactions require explicit wallet confirmation
- Free-tier ads use an advertising ID (Google AdMob); Premium is ad-free

**Requirements**
- Android 8.0+
- A deployed governance contract set OR use demo mode
- A compatible wallet app for on-chain actions

**Keywords:** DAO, governance, voting, blockchain, Ethereum, smart contracts, treasury, decentralized, web3, proposals
