## Description
GOVDAO puts on-chain governance in your pocket. Connect your wallet and participate in your member-led organization from anywhere.

VOTE ON-CHAIN
Cast binding votes with a verifiable receipt. Read live proposals straight from the Governor contract, with real vote tallies and state.

RUN THE LIFECYCLE
Create proposals, queue succeeded ones into the timelock, and execute them — the full governance loop, from your phone, confirmed with Face ID.

TREASURY & SAFETY
View balances, spend caps, and timelocked spend requests. Rehearse the emergency guardian. Track everything in a governance calendar with reminders.

INSIGHT
A delegate analytics dashboard, an activity feed with CSV/JSON export, and quick-jump search across every screen.

FREE & PREMIUM
Free includes full transparency — proposals, voting, treasury, activity. Premium removes ads and unlocks analytics, the deploy wizard, guardian drills, and export.

Requires a deployed governance contract set, or explore everything in built-in demo mode.
