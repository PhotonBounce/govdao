What it does

Full lifecycle governance
in your pocket

Every action — from drafting a proposal to executing a treasury transfer — is signed by your wallet and settled on Polygon. No middleman. No trust required.

📋

Propose

Draft and submit on-chain governance proposals with calldata-encoded actions. When a proposal passes, its actions execute automatically through the timelock.

🗳️

Vote On-Chain

Cast For / Against / Abstain votes with your MetaMask or WalletConnect wallet. Every vote is an immutable transaction on Polygon.

⏱️

Timelock Security

Passed proposals enter a 2-day timelock. This delay gives members time to react to any malicious actions before they execute — governance safety net built-in.

💰

Treasury Transparency

Live on-chain POL balance reads, 30-day spending caps, per-transfer limits, and a full movement audit trail. Every number you see is read directly from the contract — no backend, no trust.

🛡️

Emergency Guardian

A multi-sig guardian can pause the Governor instantly in a crisis. Can only pause, never spend. Unpause requires full community vote.

📊

Analytics Suite

Voter participation heatmaps, sentiment pulse, delegate power map, and proposal risk analysis — premium insights for serious DAOs.

📱

Mobile-First

React Native app built for Android. MetaMask deep-link support and biometric confirmation before every wallet action.

🔒

Hybrid Governance — Off-chain speed, On-chain finality

GOVDAO bridges fast off-chain coordination (gasless motions and reviews) with immutable on-chain anchoring. Your team moves fast; the blockchain keeps the record.
