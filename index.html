What it does

Full lifecycle governance
in your pocket

Every action — from drafting a proposal to executing a treasury transfer — is signed by your wallet and settled on Polygon.

📋

Propose

Draft and submit on-chain governance proposals. Calldata-encoded actions execute automatically when passed.

🗳️

Vote

Cast For / Against / Abstain votes with MetaMask or WalletConnect. Votes are recorded on-chain.

⏱️

Timelock

Passed proposals queue in a 2-day timelock, giving members time to react before execution.

💰

Treasury

Live on-chain POL balance, 30-day spending caps, and a full transfer audit trail.

🛡️

Emergency Guardian

Multi-sig guardian can pause the Governor in a crisis. Unpause requires community consensus.

📱

Mobile-first

React Native app with MetaMask deep-link on Android. Web app works in any browser.
