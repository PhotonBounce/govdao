# Commercialization

## Product Packaging

GOVDAO should be sold in three layers:

1. Core protocol: open source credibility layer.
2. Hosted control plane: indexing, notifications, metadata pinning, analytics, audit exports.
3. Mobile and branded client: the interface institutions and communities actually deploy.

That product can be sold in two operating modes:

1. on-chain governance workspace for high-assurance treasury and proposal flows
2. hybrid workspace that combines auditable on-chain decisions with off-chain motions, document approvals, meeting workflows, and companion productivity modules

## Who Pays

- municipalities and oversight groups that need transparent voting records
- associations and unions that need member governance
- co-ops and community networks that need treasury transparency
- companies that want auditable internal governance for committees or spending approvals
- organizations that want one member app combining governance with document review, chat, support, or operations workflows

## Pricing Direction

- free developer tier for testnet deployments
- paid team tier for hosted notifications, search, and support
- enterprise tier for branding, SSO, SLAs, procurement, and managed deployments
- hybrid governance tier for off-chain motion workflows, hosted records, passkey auth, and companion business modules

## Why Someone Buys Instead Of Forks

- security-reviewed default contract stack
- ready-to-publish mobile client configuration
- hybrid DAO flows that cover decisions which should not always settle on-chain
- super-app style module bundling that reduces app sprawl for members and operators
- compliance-safe deployment playbooks
- incident response and governance operations support
- institutional onboarding and migration support

## Sales Message

Sell this as verifiable governance software:

- proposals are public and tamper-evident
- votes are recorded on-chain
- treasury changes are timelocked
- emergency powers are narrow and auditable
- hosted services are optional convenience, not hidden control points
