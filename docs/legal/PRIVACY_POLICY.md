[LEGAL ENTITY NAME] ("we", "us") operates the GOVDAO mobile application (the "App"). This policy describes what information the App handles and how.

## What the App does

GOVDAO is a governance client. It displays proposals, votes, treasury state, and emergency-guardian status for blockchain-based organizations, and lets authorized members sign governance actions.

## Information we process

- **Wallet addresses.** When you connect a wallet or passkey-linked identity, the App processes your public wallet address to display your membership role and to construct transactions you explicitly sign. Private keys never leave your wallet; the App cannot access them.
- **On-chain data.** Proposals, votes, and treasury activity are read from public blockchain networks. Anything you submit on-chain (including votes) is public and permanent by the nature of the underlying network — the App cannot delete it.
- **Off-chain governance content.** If your organization enables hosted services (document rooms, motions, notifications), content you submit there is stored by the service operator identified in your deployment configuration: [SERVICE OPERATOR + REGION].
- **Support contact.** If you email support, we process your email address and message to respond.

## What the App does not do

- No advertising or third-party ad SDKs.
- No analytics SDKs collecting behavioral data. [DELETE IF UNTRUE]
- No access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or files.
- No sale of personal information.

## Data retention and deletion

Off-chain service data is retained per your organization's configuration and can be deleted on request to [CONTACT]. On-chain data cannot be altered or deleted by anyone, including us.

## Your choices

You may disconnect your wallet at any time from the in-app session card. Disconnecting removes the local session; it does not affect data already recorded on-chain.
